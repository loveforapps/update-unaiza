Just a simple human being obsessing over love, who believes that love can change lives. I'm a media student at Kinnaird College for women's University by day and a writer by the sunset. And maybe messy hair, sunset eyes, cold rain, tea and crunchy leaves alone explain my life. Warm days, unkind people and crowds paint me all over in blue. I believe in kindness, the devils of our youth and stardust on skin. I am all about writing custom poems for you and your loved ones to make your day better, to make your loved one smile or to add a handful of happiness to your special day. I might not be the best writer out there but probably the coolest! and I write Urdu poetry, too. Unaiza
